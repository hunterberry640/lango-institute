Welcome to

LanGo Institute

language learning on the go

LanGo Institute is a language education center in Dallas, Texas dedicated to making language learning effective, enjoyable, and rooted in real linguistic science. We offer group classes, private lessons, corporate training, workshops, and international immersion trips in Spanish, Korean, Japanese, Mandarin, French, German, Portuguese, and more.

Our Approach

Let's get linguisticky.

At LanGo, we don't just teach you phrases — we teach you how language works. Our workshops give you real linguistic tools to make your language learning more effective, more insightful, and a whole lot more fun.

From phonetics to morphology to syntax, you'll learn the science that powers fluency.
